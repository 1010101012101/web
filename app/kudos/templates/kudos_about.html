{% trans "What is Kudos?" %}

{% trans "Kudos is a way of showing your appreciation for another Gitcoin member. It's also a way to showcase special skills that a member might have, such as Pythonista, or Design Star. Kudos tokens can also be bought and sold on the Kudos Marketplace." %}

{% trans "How Do I Get One?" %}

{% trans "Kudos can be gifted to you by any other Gitcoin member. You can also purchase Kudos through the marketplace and send it to any other Gitcoin user. When you send a Kudos, they receive an email notication. After they accept the Kudos, it will be minted on the blockchain and delivered to its recepient." %}
